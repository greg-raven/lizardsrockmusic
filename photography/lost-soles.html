Lost Soles

Photographs of my Lost Soles exhibit was on display at the Business Resource Innovation Center (BRIC) in Carson City (108 E. Proctor Street) from November 2011 through December 2012. The exhibit features kid’s shoes I found on Del Monte Beach in Monterey, CA, over 21 years of beach-walking. No shoe was collected unless it was alone.

— Nancy
