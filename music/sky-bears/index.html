Sky Bears / Songs for the Holidays

Available through the iTunes Music Store. (You can download iTunes for Mac or PC here.)

This CD is a lively, thoughtful collection of international winter festival music from two classic Nancy Raven audio tapes: Sky Bears, and Songs for the Holidays. From the bouyant energy in the African Thanksgiving song to the plaintive story of Sz Bbei, the Chinese farmer who cannot go to the New Year’s festival, you will find many songs and celebrations for children to enjoy.

Engineered by Mike Cogan, Bay Records, Alameda, California
