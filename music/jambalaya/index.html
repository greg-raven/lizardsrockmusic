Nancy Raven’s

Jambalaya!

In this tasty collection of food songs from around the world, Nancy Raven, joined by other musicians, has found words and concepts that are fast disappearing in our techno-crazy world. The sound is lively and fun, field and street hollers, combined with songs and singing games make this a great recording. It features Nancy’s song about how kids can help their families prepare for an earthquake.
