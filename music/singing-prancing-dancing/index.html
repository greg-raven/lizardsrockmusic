Nancy Raven’s

Singing, Prancing, & Dancing

(Winner of the Parents’ Choice Award, 1990 and 1991)

Music of Malvina Reynolds and Woodie Guthrie for children. This award-winning recording contains seventeen attractively-arranged children’s favorites. Nancy’s vocals demonstrate her own enjoyment of these songs, and carry the listener right along with her. The enthusiastic appealing backup band includes guitar, banjo, kazoo, percussion, vocal harmonies, and sound effects, for a varied fare everyone will enjoy.

Songs on this CD are:

  1. Mail Myself to You (Woody Guthrie)
  2. If You Love Me (Malvina Reynolds)
  3. Place to Be (Malvina Reynolds)
  4. Come See (Woody Guthrie)
  5. Morningtown Ride (Malvina Reynolds)
  6. Dance Around (Woody Guthrie)
  7. Upside Down (Malvina Reynolds)
  8. Turtle Song (Malvina Reynolds)
  9. Hanuka Dance (Woody Guthrie)
  10. Car Song (Woody Guthrie)
  11. Lambeth Children (Malvina Reynolds)
  12. Don’t You Push Me (Woody Guthrie)
  13. Howdja Do (Woody Guthrie)
  14. I Live in a City (Malvina Reynolds)
  15. Merry Go Round (Woody Guthrie & Marjorie Mazia)
  16. Count Ten (Malvina Reynolds)
  17. One of the Family (Malvina Reynolds)
